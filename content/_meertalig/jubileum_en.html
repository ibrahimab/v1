The hobby that got out of control
What started in 1999 as a hobby that got out of control, has now grown to a fully fledged travel company. Today Chalet.nl is specialized in the rental of chalets and apartments from 2 to 44 persons, particularly in France, Austria and Switzerland.

In the summer season you can also book via us. Soon we will launch a specialized website for this: Zomerhuisje.nl.

Search & Book
We would love to help you with your search for a good accommodation. To make this even easier, we have made some changes to the website. You will now find the search & book function on our homepage and the descriptions and pictures of the accommodations have received a better spot.
