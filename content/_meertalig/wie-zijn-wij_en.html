Chalet.nl is an independent tour operator specialized in the rental of detached chalets and luxurious apartments from 2 until 50 persons in France, Austria, Switzerland, Italy and Germany.

The organisation was founded in 1999 as a sole proprietorship and has grown to a fully fledged travel company with a team of twenty-two people. We are located in Woerden, in the province of Utrecht in the Netherlands.
